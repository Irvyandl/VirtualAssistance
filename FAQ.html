Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer as a Virtual Assistant?

I provide administrative support, data visualization (Excel/Power BI), and visual content (PowerPoint/Canva). I handle email management, calendar coordination, CRM tasks, reports, dashboards, and branded presentations.

How do I know you're reliable and organized?

Every task is tracked, reported, and scheduled through organized tools like Google Calendar and Excel. My work process includes weekly updates and structured delivery cycles, so everything is clear and consistent.

Do you handle confidential data securely?

Yes. I use encrypted sharing platforms like Google Drive and OneDrive, password managers when needed, and am open to NDAs for sensitive data.

How do payments work?

All payments are securely managed via Escrow.com. This protects both parties by holding funds until work is approved. You'll receive an official Escrow link where we both agree on terms, and you stay in control every step of the way.

How do we get started?

Once you fill out the client form, I’ll review your needs and suggest a personalized service plan. From there, we’ll confirm scope and timelines, and you’ll receive your secure Escrow link. Work begins once terms are approved.
